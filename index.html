Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Little Tree Capital.

Is this tax or legal advice?

No. Little Tree Capital provides educational tools and information. We are not licensed to provide tax, legal, or investment advice. Please consult qualified professionals for advice specific to your situation.

Do you store my statement uploads?

No. This demo does not store any files you upload. Your documents remain on your device. When our full platform launches, any data handling will be clearly disclosed with your consent.

What kinds of investments do you support?

Our calculator tools work with mutual funds, GICs, ETFs, and other common investment products. Our core offering is a 6% fixed return, tax-exempt investment structured as interest on a note.

Can band councils use this?

Yes. Little Tree Capital is designed to serve individuals, businesses, trusts, and band councils. We have specific views and reports designed for committee and trustee presentations.

How do you get paid?

Little Tree Capital retains a spread between the returns generated by our institutional investments and the fixed rate paid to investors. This is disclosed transparently - no hidden fees.
